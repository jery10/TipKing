The service

Predict4Free is a free football prediction platform. Users make predictions on upcoming matches and receive tips (cash rewards in Nigerian Naira) for correct predictions. No deposit or payment is ever required to participate.

Eligibility

You must be 18 or older to use Predict4Free. By registering you confirm you are of legal age in your jurisdiction.

Predictions & payouts

Predictions are locked at kickoff. Results are settled by Predict4Free administrators after matches complete. Current payout structure:

  • Match Result correct — ₦100
  • Exact Score correct — ₦1,600
  • Over/Under (2.5, 3.5, or 4.5) correct — ₦50 each
  • Goals Range correct — ₦200
  • Both Teams to Score correct — ₦200

Predict4Free reserves the right to adjust the payout structure with advance notice.

Payout process

Earnings are credited to your Predict4Free balance automatically once a match is settled. Payouts are processed within 7 days of a match being settled. Payment is made directly to the Nigerian bank account saved in your profile. A minimum balance of ₦500 is required before a payout can be processed. Predict4Free is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect bank details provided by the user.
