The service

TipKing is a free football prediction platform. Users make predictions on upcoming matches and receive tips (cash rewards in Nigerian Naira) for correct predictions. No deposit or payment is ever required to participate.

Predictions are locked at kickoff. Results are settled by TipKing administrators after matches complete.
