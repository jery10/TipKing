🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

We collect your email address, username, and optional X/Twitter handle when you register. We also store the predictions you make (teams, scores, reasoning) and your prediction results.

How we use it

Your email is used only to log you in — it is never shown publicly. Your username and predictions are displayed on the leaderboard and match pages. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Cookies & sessions

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the EU. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Your predictions will be removed from the leaderboard within 7 days.

Contact

Questions? Reach us via X/Twitter or through the app.
